Nike Trail

Advertisement/poster designs for the Nike Trail series. Completed: 6/28/2023

About The Project

This unofficial project for Nike aims to design a series of visually captivating and engaging advertisements for Nike's trail running shoes that appeal to outdoor enthusiasts. The advertisements showcase the vibrant colors of the color wheel (red, orange, yellow, green, light blue, dark blue, purple, and pink) to create a sense of excitement and energy. The poster advertisements seamlessly incorporate brutalist-inspired aesthetics into their advertisement posters, resulting in a strikingly modern and organized feel.

The use of geometric shapes and stark contrasts creates a sense of visual impact that is sure to grab the attention of potential customers. Overall, the advertisements are a bold and stylish way to showcase Nike's trail running shoes. They are sure to appeal to outdoor enthusiasts who are looking for a shoe that can keep up with their active lifestyles.

Problem and Solution

In my design process, I faced two challenges: crafting effective compositions within the chosen style and ensuring uniqueness among designs with shared stylistic elements. To tackle the first challenge, I sought inspiration from platforms like Pinterest and Behance, refining my layouts. For the second challenge, I diversified the themes of the last four designs—underwater, space, and graffiti—aligning them with the shoe's characteristics. This approach maintained a cohesive overall style while giving each design a distinct identity.
