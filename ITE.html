Intelligent Tech Engine

Brand identity, logo design, and 3D art for a tech & AI consulting company. Completed: 9/15/2025

Rebranding Intelligent Tech Engine: Building a Human-Centered Future

Intelligent Tech Engine (ITE) helps e-commerce brands connect their tools, automate workflows, and fix broken processes with contemporary tech and AI. But their old brand was vague, and it looked too much like a religious org, leaving customers confused and asking, “What does ITE even do?”

That lack of clarity made them harder to trust. This rebrand solved the problem by putting people, not hype, at the center, creating a simple, premium identity with clear messaging and a consistent design system. The result is a brand that reflects ITE’s true mission: “Where tech thinks like humans do.”
