About The Projects

Here's a collection of posters I've created over the years,driven by my passion for design. Through creating these engaging passion projects, I immersed myself in exploring design principles such as typography,layout, and hierarchy. I started making them in 2022, so you can see how I've improved and changed as a designer. The projects here are a bit unconventional and experimental, just like any personal project from a designer.

Why I LOVE Making Poster Designs

I love making these random and weird poster designs simply because they are fun. Most of these are just personal projects so I can do whatever I want with them without any constraints. Sometimes we just have to design just for the sake of designing.
