I'm a Calgary-based designer who treats design like oxygen; it's not a career I clock into, it's my way of life. With 3 years of experience in brand and graphic design, I push brands into bold, unexpected territory where they actually stand out.

Unlike the "trendy" one-style-fits-all designers flooding Instagram, I don't box myself in. Sticking to one look is boring, and brands deserve more than a "style". I can build something premium, clean, and modern one day, then craft a brand identity that feels like it belongs on the cover of a black metal record the next. My versatility is the point: design should serve the brand, not my personal aesthetic.

My work is built on high standards and an obsession with details most people overlook, because a brand doesn't just need to look good, it needs to feel right. Having majored in Graphic Design at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology, I bring precision, strategy, and a refusal to settle into mediocrity in every project. Safe design is forgettable design. And forgettable isn't an option in this industry; it's a crime.

Outside of visual design I love working out in the gym, listening to metal, doing mountain hikes, designing Geometry Dash levels, learning about Dinosaurs/paleontology, and diving deep into dark internet rabbit holes (waiting for the OG JTK imageü§û).
