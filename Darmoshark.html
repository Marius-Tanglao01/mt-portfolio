Darmoshark M3

Advertisement designs for a gaming mouse brand.

Exploring the Language of Premium Gaming

Darmoshark is a high-end gaming peripherals brand from China, best known for its innovation and premium quality. For this visual exploration, I developed advertising designs for their flagship M3 gaming mouse, including the 4K edition. The challenge was to elevate the brand’s visual language with a consistent premium aesthetic that matched the sophistication of the product itself.

My solution was to craft an abstract tech style that fused clean gradients, bold geometric shapes, and futuristic overlays, highlighting both the M3’s lightweight design and its powerful precision. What began as a small design exercise evolved into a deeper exploration of how to visually express performance and innovation at a premium level.
