Darmoshark M3

Advertisement designs for a gaming mouse brand.

Exploring the Language of Premium Gaming

Darmoshark is known for high-performance gaming peripherals, but its visual identity often leaned toward a mid-market aesthetic, limiting its perception as a premium brand. This advertising project focused on repositioning the brand’s flagship M3 gaming mouse into something more refined and elevated.

The Approach Centred on Fast Clarity

A reduced color palette, bold sans-serif typography, and controlled geometric compositions were used to shift the tone from energetic to precise and intentional. Clean gradients and maximal visuals allowed the product’s form and performance to take priority.

The Goal Wasn’t to Make Them Just Pretty

It was to make them sharper. Rather than emphasizing technical features with jargon, the visuals were designed to communicate confidence and technical precision. The result is a more cohesive visual language that aligns the product with a premium audience.
