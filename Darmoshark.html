Darmoshark M3

Advertisement designs for a gaming mouse brand. Completed: 12/6/2023

About The Project

Darmoshark, a gaming peripherals brand headquartered in China, specializes in delivering premium products tailored for the high-end gaming market. Renowned for innovation, Darmoshark pioneers gaming excellence with a diverse range of high-quality gaming products.

This project centers on the creation of advertising designs for Darmoshark's flagship product, namely the Darmoshark M3 gaming mouse, including its 4k edition. This is an unofficial passion project that started out as a small design exercise but it slowly turned into a bigger and more complex creative exploration.

The Objectives

This project aimed to craft distinctive advertisement designs for the Darmoshark M3 mouse, aiming to convey a sense of sophistication, high-end, and premium quality. I identified a need for an elevated aesthetic in the existing designs of the company. Furthermore, my goal was to visually articulate the dual nature of the mouse - portraying it not only as a lightweight marvel but also as a powerful and precise gaming tool.

Problem and Solution

I encountered the challenge of establishing a consistent premium aesthetic across all advertisements. Following thoughtful consideration, I opted for an abstract tech style characterized by clean gradients, bold shapes, and tech overlays to effectively convey the desired visual aesthetic. My other challenge was executing the idea, which can be seen in the process.

The Process (Behind the Scenes)

Below are some sketches I created to brainstorm different compositions. I played around with unique shapes for the backgrounds, embracing a more unconventional approach. The drawings may seem random, but that's part of the creative brainstorming process. This experimentation helped me refine and move closer to my vision.

The Process Continued

After the experimental sketching phase, I chose to bring the ideas to life in Photoshop. I utilized tools such as the pen and gradient tools and incorporated some technology-related stock photos.
